# 90's Pong
Nostalgic clone of "Pong" with strange sound effects!

How to Play

1. This game is co-op
2. The first one to reach 20 points, wins the game.
3. Slide the paddles up/down to block the ball, and score into your opponent's side

Controls:
-Press F to enter full screen
-Press escape or the "X" button of the screen to close the window

Player 1:
-Press W to slide the left paddle up
-Press S to slide the left paddle down
Player 2:
-Press the Up Arrow key to slide the right paddle up
-Press the Down Arrow key to slide the right paddle down
